Extraction Features

Natural Language Instructions

Use plain English instructions to extract information without needing to specify patterns. Simply describe what you're looking for, such as "Find the invoice total" or "Extract the customer's full address".

  • Extracts information based on meaning, not just patterns
  • Adapts to different document formats and layouts
  • Understands context and semantics of the document

Pattern-Based Extraction

For more precise extraction needs, define specific patterns to match in your documents. Use exact text matching, regular expressions, or extract text that follows specific patterns.

  • Exact pattern matching with context awareness
  • Regular expression support for complex patterns
  • Extract text before, after, or between patterns
